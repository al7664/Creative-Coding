Scientific Name:

Smogobo

Discovered by / at:

Sihfu Adz

Family:

Smogoborouas: Smogo is the first and last creature of the family as it has an infinite lifeline and doesn't need correlation with others for survival. Adorable yet harmful it has a lonely yet fun life spreading pollution in the air clan. Smogo represents the powerfulness of pollution and its toxicity to objects around it.

Habitat:

Air Clan

Appearance:

Smogo emobies a circular grayscale body structure with a pair of fierce eyebrows to match the antagonist role it plays in the world./p >

Key Characteristics:

The key characteristic of Smogo is that its existence creates pollution. Whereve Smogo goes, it leaves a trail as in this world as pollution is irreversible.

  • Fierce Look
  • Color Change
  • Indefinite Trail from Movement
  • Increases Surrounding Pollution

What we know so far about Smogo: Smogo's presence in the air clan is similar to pollution in our current world. The larger Smogo spreads and exists, the colorful world around it slowly gets covered. Smogo naturally spreads grayness in the air clan, and through interactions, Smogo can spread even faster.
Guidelines for human interaction with Smogo:Smogo represents that more human interactions create more pollution. The longer the user interacts with Smogo, or utilize the keys and mouse to expand Smogo, the larger Smogo becomes, therefore spreading pollution.
